About Food Vision

Food Vision is a deep learning project using computer vision and transfer learning techniques to identify food items and predict their calories.

Our model is trained on the Food-101 dataset and uses EfficientNet architecture for accurate predictions.

Key Features:

Accurate food identification
Calorie estimation per serving
Support for multiple image formats
User history tracking

Model Architecture:

EfficientNet architecture used in our model

EfficientNet's layers use a compound scaling method that uniformly scales the depth, width, and resolution of the network for optimal performance.
